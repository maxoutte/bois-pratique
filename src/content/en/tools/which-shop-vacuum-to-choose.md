Dust collection usually gets delayed too long. That is understandable, because it feels less exciting than buying another cutting tool. But the comfort of a workshop depends heavily on dust control once sanding, routing and repeat cutting become normal.

## What to avoid

The classic bad purchase is either:

- a tiny vacuum that becomes decorative rather than useful
- or an oversized setup bought far too early for the workshop it serves

The right choice depends on the machines you plan to connect and the level of comfort you want to reach.
