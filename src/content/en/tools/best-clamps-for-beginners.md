Clamps are one of the least glamorous purchases in a beginner workshop, which is exactly why people often get them wrong. They buy too few, buy random assortments or buy purely on price. Then they wonder why assemblies drift, guides move and glue-ups become irritating.

## What to buy first

The smartest start is not “more clamps.” It is the right kinds of clamps for the jobs that keep coming back.
