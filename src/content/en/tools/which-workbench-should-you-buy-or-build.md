Workbenches carry a lot of symbolic weight in woodworking. Many people want a “real bench” before they even know what role it needs to play. But a good starter bench does not need to be legendary. It needs to be stable, sensible for the space and genuinely useful.

## Buy or build?

### Buy
A good option if you want to save time and already know what you need from the top, stability and storage.

### Build
A very logical option if you want something simple, sized to your workshop, and useful as a learning project in itself.
