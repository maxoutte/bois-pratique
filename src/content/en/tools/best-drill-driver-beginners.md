Drill drivers are easy to take for granted because they seem obvious. In practice, they are often the most-used power tool in a beginner shop. Pre-drilling, driving screws, fixing guides, assembling storage and correcting mistakes all run through this one tool, so a weak purchase gets noticed quickly.

## What matters most

For a first drill driver, I care about:

- balance in the hand
- predictable control during screw driving
- the logic of the battery platform
- chuck quality
- overall credibility rather than kit marketing
