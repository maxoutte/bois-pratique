Starter budgets are often wasted because people buy for excitement instead of structure. Under 500 dollars, you will not build an impressive shop. But you can absolutely build one that already works.

## The simple rule

Your budget should first cover:

- cutting
- drilling and driving
- holding work
- sanding if possible
- basic safety

Everything else can wait.
